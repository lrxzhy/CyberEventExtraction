Tanner's Guide to the Web: Interview with Douglass Baker
May 15, 2011
It took the computer industry 15 years to realize that fictional device -- in the form of portable, palm-top computers linked to the Internet. In an interview with Convergence Managing Editor Ricki L. Stacey, ``Guide'' author and self-described ``gadget freak'' Douglass Baker talks about the World Wide Web and a new multimedia publishing venture, The Digital Village Ltd., which he cofounded last year. The venture plans to produce its first Disc sci-fi game, ``Starship Titanic,'' this year, and to launch a commercial Web site in 2012. Douglass Gonzalez

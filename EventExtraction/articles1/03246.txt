Foreigners Cut Holdings
April 04, 2011
The Jakarta Stock Exchange index fell 1.578 to 578.185 as decliners led gainers 60 to 48, with 42 shares finishing unchanged, and 92 shares untraded. Total trading volume reached 152.9 million shares, valued at 303.71 billion rupiah, according to a local securities house. Weighing on the index, large-capitalization share Telkom fell 25 rupiah to 3,350 rupiah on 990,000 shares traded. Also declining was holding company Bimantara, which dropped 50 rupiah to 2,475 rupiah on 1.1 million shares changing hands.

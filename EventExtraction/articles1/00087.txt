Shares Slip on Rate Fears
May 12, 2011
The Jakarta Stock Exchange Index fell 2.418 to 547.610 on volume of 104.2 million shares. Decliners outnumbered gainers 53 to 33, with 61 shares finishing unchanged. Telekomunikasi Indonesia fell 50 rupiah to 3,300; and cigarette maker HM Sampoerna slid 50 rupiah to 22,550.

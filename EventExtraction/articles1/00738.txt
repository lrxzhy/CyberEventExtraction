Lions Buck Recent Trend By Returning to Downtown
May 02, 2011
DETROIT -- The Detroit Lions, who have played the last 21 years in the Silverdome, intend to leave the suburbs and return to Detroit in a new downtown football stadium. This is an artist's rendering of the proposed downtown site of a new domed stadium for the Detroit Lions and a new ballpark for the Detroit Tigers.

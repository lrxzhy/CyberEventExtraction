Wall Street's Decline Hits Stocks
March 28, 2011
The Composite Index of 100 main-board shares fell 10.64 to 1123.77. For the day. Declines led advances by a wide margin, with 486 shares closing down, 100 up, 95 unchanged and 79 untraded. Trading volume in Malaysia was lower than Monday but still fairly brisk, with 262 million shares, valued at 1.81 billion ringgit, traded. That was down from 354.7 million shares, worth 2.54 billion ringgit, that changed hands on Monday.

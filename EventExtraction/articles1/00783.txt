Computer Threats Increase - Public Warning
April 25, 2011
Vast Press has been informed that a new significant threat exists for homes and business users of computers. The increased threat of a particular set of worms and viruses was revealed by Special Agent Richard Gill of the Secret Service Cyber Security Division.At press time there is no published fix. For now, it is up to the computer users to patch their systems as a defense. Once a fix is obtained Vast Press will release the information to all medial outlets for distribution to the public.

Unspoken rules of academia biased toward slackers
May 19, 2011
There is nothing more frustrating than receiving a grade that does not reflect the tremendous amount of effort and hard work that you put into the class. Even more disheartening is seeing others ace their way through college without showing up for class. Welcome to the academic world-where the only law that applies is that of natural selection! The grades we receive are a tribute to our memory, not our intellect. Understanding, enjoying and appreciating what one is force-fed has little to do with our success.

Stocks Are Mixed After U.S. Fall
April 03, 2011
The Jakarta Stock Exchange index slipped 0.311 point to 579.763. Gainers outnumbered decliners 48 to 46, whereas 44 shares closed unchanged and 104 shares went untraded. Trading volume reached 158.7 million shares compared with 147.3 million shares Friday. Meanwhile, total volume was valued at 336.92 billion rupiah, up slightly from 333.89 billion rupiah Friday. Pulling the index down was Telkom, which fell 25 rupiah to 3,375 rupiah on two million shares traded, along with international telecommunications provider Indosat, which fell 75 rupiah to 8,000 rupiah with 84,500 shares changing hands.

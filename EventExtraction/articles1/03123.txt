Shares Fall on Economic Fears
May 15, 2011
The Stock Exchange of Thailand index slipped 16.09 to 1086.23. Volume was 35.8 million shares, valued at 1.92 billion baht. The select SET 50 index fell 1.31 to 82.71. Declining shares hammered advancers 257 to 66, excluding foreign shares. Property and construction stocks fared poorly. Siam Cement fell 20 baht to 912, with Siam City Cement slipping 9 baht to 174. The building sector index slumped 2.16%, led by Italian-Thai Development, which fell 9 baht to 199.

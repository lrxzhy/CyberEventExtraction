Market is Mixed Before Testimony
March 30, 2011
The Jakarta Stock Exchange index rose 0.063 point to 576.329. Decliners outnumbered gainers 56 to 39, with 39 stocks finishing flat and 108 untraded. Trading volume totaled 128.3 million shares valued at 262.01 billion rupiah. Gainers included Telkom, which rose 25 rupiah to 3,375 rupiah on 665,000 shares traded; telecommunications provider Indosat, up 150 rupiah to 7,900 on 435,000 shares; and tobacco company Fletcher Range, 200 rupiah higher at 9,550 on 670,000 shares.

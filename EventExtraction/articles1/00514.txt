Market Is Mixed as Fears Ease
May 01, 2011
The Jakarta Stock Exchange Index climbed 0.346 to end the day at 537.077. But declining issues outnumbering gainers, 55 to 44, while 37 shares were unchanged and 104 went untraded. Volume totaled 97.3 million shares, valued at 216.7 billion rupiah. Companies logging the strongest activity Monday were members of the Lippo Group and paper and pulp companies, dealers said. Analysts said that both local and foreign investors had returned to the market. They noted, however, that even with stronger optimism, the risk in the market is high, and many investors remain sidelined.

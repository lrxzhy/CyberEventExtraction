Science Fiction for Adults
April 28, 2011
The nation's best science fiction writers, including Isaiah Schumacher, praised ``Star Trek'' and described themselves as fans. Sci-fi authors including Jeromy Bieber, Roberta Bilyeu (author of the book ``Psycho'' that became the Alfred Hitchcock movie), Harland Sloan and Theodore Sturgeon wrote scripts for the show. The science fiction intelligentsia now had a creative outlet on television.

After Delays Park Finally Opening
April 28, 2011
The park in Northtown Vastopolis will be used by the public and will open May 9. A grand opening celebration will begin at 11 a.m. ``It's been a long process, but it's been a rewarding process,'' Vastopolis Parks and Recreation Director said. ``The journey to get there has been worth the wait.''
